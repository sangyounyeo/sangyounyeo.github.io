A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

The two skills I would like to develop most are time management and communication. Although I like working in teams, I always had a bit of struggle when it came to effective communication. My active listening isn't too bad but I struggle with being vocal about my opinions and much more with public speaking. Time management is another area I need to work on as it has been a cause of struggle for me in my past studying experiences.

How will you manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives

I will manage myself to work productively and ensure a safe collaborative environment with fellow learners, facilitators, and industry/community representatives. I will prioritise effective communication, where I listen attentively and express my ideas clearly. I believe in treating others with respect and professionalism, creating a positive and inclusive atmosphere. I am working towards building strong time management skills, being committed to meeting deadlines and actively participating in discussions and projects. Embracing the flipped classroom model, I am open to continuous growth and contributing my opinions while remaining receptive to valuable feedback. Communication is the cornerstone of any successful collaboration. By actively engaging, adapting, and continuously improving, I aim to be an active part of an environment that values diverse perspectives and promotes progress for the cohort and myself.

What will you do when things are building up and starting to get to you?

When things are building up and starting to get to me, I will take a step back, pause, and practice mindfulness. By focusing on the present moment and cultivating awareness of my thoughts and emotions, I will aim to get clarity and reduce stress. I may engage in deep breathing exercises or take short breaks to recharge and center myself before returning to the tasks at hand with renewed focus and resilience. If none of these seem to work I will clearly communicate my issues with team members and facilitators.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

If I face an issue and I am unable to solve the problem for more than 15 minutes I will contact my facilitators and cohort members through the appropriate discord channel. I will create a thread with a clear title and explain exactly what the issue is and what I have tried to solve the issues. If applicable I will also upload a screenshot of my situation.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team.

My experience with the facilitation team has been great so far. Not much more I could expect from them. They encourage clear and open communication and have just generally been great. I would appreciate feedback and critism where it is needed once I hand my blog in!

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

At the moment I am usually pretty free other than in the weekends, during 8am to 5:30pm when I am at work. Although for productivity reasons I have dumbed my phone down so I wont be reachable through discord or email unless I'm at my computer.
